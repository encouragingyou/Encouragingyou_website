# Prompt 39 To 40 Handover

Prompt 39 establishes the route-level SEO contract and the crawlable shell-link layer.

## What is now stable

- `site/src/content/seo/default.json` is the explicit route-intent and indexability register.
- `site/src/lib/content/site-seo.ts` is the canonical metadata builder.
- `site/src/layouts/BaseLayout.astro` now emits canonical, robots, Open Graph, and Twitter tags from one shared metadata object.
- `site/src/content/shellConfig/default.json` now carries related-route groups for deliberate internal linking.
- `site/src/components/site/ShellWayfinding.astro` renders breadcrumbs, back links, notices, and related links together.
- `site/scripts/validate-seo-metadata.mjs` is the metadata/indexability validator.
- `site/tests/seo-metadata.test.mjs` and `site/tests/e2e/contracts/seo-metadata.spec.mjs` cover the new contract.

## What Prompt 40 should assume

- self-canonical URLs are deliberate and should remain the default
- legal/trust utility routes are intentionally `noindex,follow`
- editorial detail routes may be current-and-indexable or reachable-but-noindex depending on `indexVisible`
- volunteer and partner routes intentionally keep their own canonicals while inheriting `Get Involved` current-nav state
- share-image fallback is already constrained by the Prompt 38 media truth model

## Watchouts

- do not replace the new metadata helper with route-local `<head>` logic
- do not let `robots.txt` or sitemap generation drift from `site/src/content/seo/default.json`
- do not emit social-preview assets that bypass media restrictions or imply documentary photography where the route still uses synthetic illustration
- if Prompt 40 introduces dedicated OG images, swap them into the metadata helper, not the page templates
