# Prompt 36 To 37 Handover

> Historical note: Prompt 43 later moved the live cookie state from pure `no-banner` / analytics absent to `informational-notice` with a first-party aggregate analytics objection control.

Prompt 36 turns `/cookies/` into a real production notice and locks the launch consent decision to `no-banner`.

## What is now stable

- `site/src/content/storageAccess/default.json` is the canonical storage/access registry and consent-state source of truth
- `site/src/pages/cookies/index.astro` is a real production route with:
  - the launch no-banner decision;
  - active and absent registry sections; and
  - change-control triggers for future analytics, embeds, or consent tooling
- `site/scripts/validate-structured-content.mjs` now cross-checks cookie/storage state against `site/src/content/privacyNotice/default.json`
- the Privacy Notice now explicitly references the Cookie Notice and the launch no-banner state

## What Prompt 37 should do

- treat `/cookies/` as production, not placeholder
- reference the no-banner state when writing broader legal and accessibility microcopy
- avoid reintroducing generic cookie-banner language into forms, footers, or notices unless the storage registry changes first
- keep accessibility and terms surfaces consistent with the current storage/access position

## Watchouts

- do not add a banner just because a legal route now exists
- do not claim consent records, preference cookies, or analytics providers that still do not exist
- if Prompt 37 needs wording about cookies, storage, or notice controls, source it from `storageAccess.settings` and `/cookies/`, not route-local prose
