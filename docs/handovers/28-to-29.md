# Prompt 28 To 29 Handover

Prompt 28 built the editorial detail-route family on top of the Prompt 27 feed.

## What is now stable

- canonical editorial detail truth still lives in `site/src/content/updatesFeed/default.json`
- the updates-feed schema now supports typed detail content, slugs, lifecycle fields, related-item ids, and CTA bands
- route generation and lifecycle derivation live in `site/src/lib/domain/editorial-feed.js`
- detail-model assembly and structured-data branching live in `site/src/lib/content/site-content.ts`
- dynamic detail routing now lives at `site/src/pages/events-updates/[slug].astro`
- reusable editorial detail UI now exists in:
  - `site/src/components/sections/EditorialDetailHero.astro`
  - `site/src/components/sections/EditorialFactRail.astro`
  - `site/src/components/sections/EditorialRelatedSection.astro`
  - `site/src/components/sections/EventDetailTemplate.astro`
  - `site/src/components/sections/EditorialUpdateDetailTemplate.astro`
- shell wayfinding now supports editorial-detail breadcrumb/back-link overrides through `site/src/layouts/BaseLayout.astro`

## What Prompt 29 should do

- build the production `Get Involved` hub on top of the now-stable opportunity/editorial family
- consume opportunity signals from the canonical editorial collection instead of inventing a second opportunity feed
- preserve the separation between editorial detail pages and operational hub pages

## Watchouts

- do not move volunteer, partner, or referral detail back into editorial routes when it belongs on the hub
- do not bypass `detailVisible` and `indexVisible` with route-local filtering
- do not add dated event schema or logistics unless real public event dates exist in source-backed content
