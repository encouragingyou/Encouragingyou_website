# Prompt 34 To 35 Handover

Prompt 34 turns safeguarding into a real route family with its own secure intake surface.

## What is now stable

- `site/src/content/safeguardingInfo/default.json` is the canonical safeguarding truth source
- `site/src/lib/domain/safeguarding-route-state.js` derives public safeguarding state
- `/safeguarding/`, `/safeguarding/child/`, and `/safeguarding/adult/` are all Astro-owned routes
- `safeguarding-concern` is a dedicated secure form surface and no longer relies on `mailto:` as the primary digital path
- child/adult detail routes inherit breadcrumbs and the `Raise a concern` shell CTA from the safeguarding family
- public trust/proof boundaries are explicit about what is published now versus still withheld

## What Prompt 35 should do

- rewrite the Privacy Notice around the now-real safeguarding, contact, volunteer, partner, and general enquiry data flows
- explain the safeguarding form data flow without inventing a CRM, SLA, or processor list that the repo does not verify yet
- align privacy language with the new safeguarding success/error and fallback copy

## Watchouts

- do not collapse safeguarding back into the ordinary contact explanation
- do not remove the safeguarding-specific privacy note from the form surface
- do not promise case-handling confidentiality or response timing in the Privacy Notice
- do not treat the public safeguarding email fallback as the only surviving route; the secure form is now live
