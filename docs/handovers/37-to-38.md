# Prompt 37 To 38 Handover

Prompt 37 turns `/accessibility/` and `/terms/` into real launch routes and introduces a canonical consent-aware helper-copy system.

## What is now stable

- `site/src/content/accessibilityStatement/default.json` is the canonical Accessibility Statement source
- `site/src/content/sitePolicy/default.json` is the canonical Terms / Site Policy source
- `site/src/content/consentAwareMicrocopy/default.json` is the shared trust-boundary helper-copy source
- `site/src/content/formSurfaces/default.json` now includes the dedicated `accessibility-feedback` surface
- `site/src/pages/accessibility/index.astro` and `site/src/pages/terms/index.astro` are real production routes, not placeholders
- `site/src/lib/content/site-content.ts` now centralizes:
  - cookie-entry-point notices
  - session download notices
  - contextual external-link notes
  - shared form status/no-JS helper copy
- `site/scripts/validate-structured-content.mjs` now enforces the new legal and microcopy alignment rules

## What Prompt 38 should do

- treat the new legal routes and helper-copy contracts as stable foundations
- expand Playwright coverage around:
  - legal route rendering and responsiveness
  - cookie no-banner wording and entry points
  - accessibility-feedback and trust-boundary states
  - disclosure behavior for external links, downloads, and future AI-illustration notices
- add any missing disclosure governance on top of `consentAwareMicrocopy` rather than reintroducing hardcoded route text

## Watchouts

- do not claim stronger accessibility certainty than `accessibilityStatement/default.json` supports
- do not reintroduce cookie-banner or preferences language while `storageAccess.settings.consentExperience` remains `no-banner`
- do not fork helper/status strings back into individual route templates
- if Prompt 38 adds new disclosure moments, extend the canonical microcopy layer first and then wire tests against that state
