# Prompt 30 - Volunteer Responsive And Structural Checklist

Prompt 30 adds the first dedicated involvement-detail checklist.

## Required surfaces at every supported viewport

- one visible H1 below the sticky header
- visible hero action into the enquiry anchor
- visible role-pathway cards
- visible safeguarding-and-checks heading
- visible time-commitment heading
- visible volunteer enquiry form
- visible privacy link near the form
- no horizontal overflow

## Narrow mobile expectations

- hero action remains reachable without media collision
- role-pathway cards collapse into a readable single-column flow
- FAQ and sidebar notice stay readable above the volunteer form
- the volunteer form anchor lands below the sticky header

## Wide desktop expectations

- role pathways read as a true multi-card comparison surface
- support/check/time sections hold as separate reading beats rather than one long stack
- FAQ and form remain distinct reading zones

## No-JS expectations

- volunteer enquiry form still renders with the volunteer reason preselected
- safeguarding link remains reachable
- FAQ disclosure remains usable
- no route-critical trust note depends on client scripting
