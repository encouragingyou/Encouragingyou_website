Privacy notice

Short, readable, and linked at the point where people share information.

When you email us or use a contact form route on this site, we use the details you share only to reply or manage your enquiry.

What we collect

  • Your name and contact details if you message us.
  • Your enquiry details so we can respond appropriately.
  • No unnecessary fields on core contact routes.

How we use it

  • To reply to your question, support request, or volunteering enquiry.
  • To arrange next steps when you ask about a session or referral.
  • Marketing updates only if you choose the separate opt-in.

Cookies and third parties

  • This launch site does not use social-feed embeds on core pages.
  • It does not set non-essential cookies by default.
  • If analytics or other non-essential cookies are added later, they should stay off until someone agrees.

Contact us about privacy

Email admin@encouragingyou.co.uk if you want to ask how your information is handled.
