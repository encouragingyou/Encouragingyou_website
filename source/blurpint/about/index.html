About EncouragingYou

Youth-led leadership with practical care built in.

EncouragingYou is designed around a simple idea: support should feel welcoming, useful, and easy to understand from the first visit.

  • Young people shape the tone, priorities, and atmosphere.
  • Practical help and confidence-building sit alongside belonging.
  • Rochdale-rooted, mobile-first, and built for low-friction access.

Who we are

A community organisation that answers user tasks before it asks for trust.

Mission

Help people build confidence, find support, and take their next step with dignity.

Approach

Use plain language, short paths, and a relationship-first welcome instead of gatekeeping.

Service area

Rochdale-focused, with sessions that feel local, reachable, and rooted in community needs.
