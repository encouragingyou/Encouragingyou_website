What to expect

Simple answers before anyone has to ask.

What happens at the CV sessions?

You can get one-to-one help with your CV, job applications, and planning your next step before youth club starts.

Is it okay if it is my first time?

Yes. We keep the language clear, the welcome calm, and the next step easy to understand before you arrive.

Is safeguarding in place?

Yes. Child and adult safeguarding are treated separately, and there is a dedicated route for concerns.
